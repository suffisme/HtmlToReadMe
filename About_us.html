What Is Bot-Army?

The curiosity to learn and contribute to the open-source community gave birth to the Bot-Army. It is an organisation of curious developers keen on developing and learning together.









   How Bot-Army came into being?

They say, one right motivation can push you to do your best. Students of Chitkara University, after attending one of the sessions of our own Microsoft Student Partner, came together to build this group of developers now known as the Bot-Army!

We aim at learning new skills and developing together. Encouraging each other to do our best.
